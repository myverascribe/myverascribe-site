A lot of questions have a quick answer

Before you write, the answer might already be here — these come up the most.

How does the license / pricing work?

$24.99 once for a full year of access — not a subscription. After the year, the app goes read-only: you can still view, search, and export your records forever, with no further payment. Renew anytime to resume adding entries. See the trial page →

Does it work on my phone or a Mac?

Desktop and laptop only for now — Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari. Mobile and tablet support is on the way. It runs inside your Google account, so any modern desktop browser works.

Is Verascribe verified by Google?

Yes. Verascribe has completed Google's OAuth verification review, so you won't see an "unverified app" notice when you authorize it — just Google's standard permission screen. Verascribe follows Google's Limited Use policy; verification confirms the permissions we request match what the app actually does. Read the privacy policy →

Can my co-parent see any of this?

No. Your records live in your own Google account, accessible only to you. There is no "co-parent side" to Verascribe — no portal for anyone else to log into.

Where do my records actually live?

In your own Google Drive — a Google Sheets workbook for your logs and a Drive folder for evidence files. We never hold them on our servers, so we can't read, lose, or withhold them. Read the privacy policy →
