## Getting Started In 1 Easy Step

<div class="rounded-2xl border border-blue-500 border-opacity-40 bg-gradient-to-tl from-blue-900 via-blue-800 to-sky-900 p-6 text-slate-100 shadow-2xl">
  <p class="text-base font-semibold uppercase tracking-wide text-blue-200">Before you launch</p>
  <div class="mt-4 space-y-6 text-base leading-relaxed text-slate-100">
    <div>
      <h4 class="mb-2 flex items-center gap-2 text-lg font-semibold text-blue-50">
        <span role="img" aria-label="Account tip">👤</span>
        Use the right Google account
      </h4>
      <p>Your records are tied to the account you sign in with. If you're signed into more than one Google account, sign out of the others first — or open the app in a private/incognito window — so the right account is used. Google doesn't always ask which one.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h4 class="mb-2 flex items-center gap-2 text-lg font-semibold text-blue-50">
        <span role="img" aria-label="Personal account">📧</span>
        Use a personal Google account
      </h4>
      <p>A personal @gmail.com works best. Work or school accounts are often blocked by an administrator.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h4 class="mb-2 flex items-center gap-2 text-lg font-semibold text-blue-50">
        <span role="img" aria-label="Bookmark">🔖</span>
        Bookmark the app to get back later
      </h4>
      <p>The app opens at app.myverascribe.com. Bookmark it now — that's how you return anytime, on any device.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h4 class="mb-2 flex items-center gap-2 text-lg font-semibold text-blue-50">
        <span role="img" aria-label="Desktop">💻</span>
        Use a desktop or laptop
      </h4>
      <p>Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari. Mobile and tablet support is coming soon.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

### Step 1: Launch the Webapp and Create Your Workbook.

<ol>
  <li>Ensure you are signed into only one Google account. This is important because you will not be able to move your workbook to a different account later.</li>
  <li>Launch the webapp at <a href="https://app.myverascribe.com" target="_blank" class="text-brand-teal hover:text-brand-teal/80 transition-colors">https://app.myverascribe.com</a></li>
  <li>Select "I'm new to Verascribe".</li>
</ol>
